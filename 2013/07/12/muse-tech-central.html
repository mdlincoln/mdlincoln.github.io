Muse Tech Central on GitHub

12 Jul 2013

Sheila Brennan notes a new listing on GutHub devoted to museums active on the open source community: Muse-tech-central.

Some neat-sounding projects include a tool four generating matching color palettes from an image (from the Cooper-Hewitt) and a framework for online publishing of scholarly catalogs (from the Indianapolis Museum of Art)
